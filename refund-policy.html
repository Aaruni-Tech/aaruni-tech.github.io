Refund Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026. This policy explains return, replacement, and refund handling for Aaruni Tech orders.

Return And Replacement Window

Customers can contact Aaruni Tech within 7 days of delivery for eligible replacement or return support if a product is damaged, defective, or different from the ordered item.

Eligibility

  • The product must be unused, with original packaging, accessories, manuals, and invoice where applicable.
  • Damage or missing items must be reported with clear photos or video proof.
  • Items damaged due to misuse, liquid damage, unauthorized repair, or normal wear are not eligible.

Refund Processing

Approved refunds are processed to the original payment method wherever possible. Refund timelines depend on the payment provider and bank, and usually take 5 to 7 business days after approval.

If replacement stock is available, Aaruni Tech may offer a replacement instead of a refund after customer confirmation.

How To Request Support

Email tech.aaruni@gmail.com or call 7842097003 with the order date, product name, issue details, and photos where required.

Cancellation

Orders can be cancelled before dispatch by contacting support. Once shipped, the return or replacement process applies.

Business Details

Refund and replacement requests are reviewed by Aaruni Tech support.
