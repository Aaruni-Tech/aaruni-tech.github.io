Shipping Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026. This policy explains order processing and delivery timelines for Aaruni Tech.

Service Area

Aaruni Tech serves customers in India. Delivery availability, charges, and timelines may vary by product, pincode, and logistics partner coverage.

Order Processing

Orders are usually processed within 1 to 2 business days after payment confirmation. Orders placed on Sundays or public holidays may be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Timeline

Most orders are delivered within 3 to 7 business days after dispatch. Remote locations, weather, holidays, or courier delays may extend this timeline.

Shipping Charges

Shipping charges, if applicable, are shown during checkout or communicated before dispatch. Aaruni Tech may offer free delivery on selected orders or promotions.

Address Accuracy

Customers are responsible for providing correct name, phone number, and delivery address. If an order cannot be delivered due to incorrect details, support will contact the customer for next steps.
